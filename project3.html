Introduction

This project involved analyzing a call centreâ€™s customer interaction data to assess call volumes, representative performance, customer satisfaction levels, and demographic trends. The goal was to develop a comprehensive, interactive dashboard using Microsoft Excel to help management make data-driven decisions for improving customer service operations.

Objective

To provide an interactive, visual reporting tool that tracks key call centre metrics such as call volumes, customer satisfaction ratings, and representative performance while uncovering operational trends based on call duration, demographics, and geography.

Methodology

  1. Data Cleaning & Pre-processing:

    Identified and handled inconsistencies, missing values, and standardized key fields like date, city, and categorical labels.

    Created new calculated columns for enhanced analysis.

  2. Descriptive & Exploratory Data Analysis:

    Built PivotTables and charts to analyze call patterns by various dimensions such as representative, city, and customer feedback.

  3. PivotTables & PivotCharts:

    Exploratory data analysis (EDA) was conducted to uncover patterns, trends, and relationships within the data. Visualization techniques such as line graphs and heatmaps were employed to illustrate these findings. Notable analyses include daily activity trends, heart rate patterns, hourly data exploration, and minute-level analysis of calorie expenditure and sleep patterns.

  4. Interactive Dashboard Design:

    Designed an intuitive dashboard layout with PivotTables, charts, and slicers for dynamic analysis and visual storytelling.

  5. Business Insights Reporting:

    Transformed operational data into actionable insights for management decision-making.

    Highlighted high-performing representatives and operational bottlenecks using easy-to-read visuals.

Results

Delivered a fully interactive Excel dashboard enabling real-time filtering and operational insights.

Management could easily monitor:

  1. Total calls handled
  2. Top representatives by satisfaction ratings
  3. Customer feedback trends

Discovered key operational trends:

  1. Certain representatives consistently achieved higher satisfaction ratings.
  2. Specific cities and days experienced higher call volumes.
  3. Longer calls tended to correlate with higher purchase amounts.

Equipped business stakeholders with a scalable, ready-to-refresh dashboard for ongoing reporting.
