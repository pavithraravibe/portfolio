Coffee Shop Sales Analysis

  2. Coffee Shop Sales Dashboard

Introduction

This project involved analyzing the sales performance of a coffee shop chain using Microsoft Excel, aiming to identify sales trends, high-performing products, and store-wise performance. The final deliverable was an interactive dashboard enabling business users to easily monitor key sales metrics and filter data dynamically.

Objective

To provide a comprehensive, visual, and interactive sales reporting solution using Excel PivotTables, PivotCharts, and Slicers â€” helping business owners track and optimize sales performance by category, location, and time.

Methodology

  1. Data Cleaning & Pre-processing:

    Reviewed and cleaned transaction data for accuracy and consistency.

    Standardized formats for dates, product categories, and store locations.

    Removed or corrected any missing, duplicate, or incorrect entries to maintain data integrity.

  2. Descriptive & Exploratory Analysis:

    Calculated key sales metrics like total sales, number of transactions, and average sales per order.

    Identified top-selling products and analyzed sales by month, day, and store location.

    Used Excel functions such as SUMIFS(), COUNTIFS(), and basic arithmetic operations for quick insights.

  3. PivotTable Creation:

    Created PivotTables to group and summarize sales data by product category, location, month, and other dimensions.

    Built PivotCharts (Bar, Column, and Pie Charts) to visualize total sales, sales by category, and store-wise performance.

  4. Dashboard Development:

    Designed an interactive Excel dashboard combining PivotTables, PivotCharts, and Slicers.

    Enabled dynamic data filtering by Product Category, Store Location, and Month without modifying the raw data.

    Focused on a clean, easy-to-navigate layout for business-friendly reporting.

  5. Business Insights Reporting:

    Translated raw sales data into meaningful, actionable business insights.

    Delivered performance trends, peak sales periods, and category-wise contributions to total sales.

    Supported decision-making for product inventory planning and promotional campaigns.

Result

1. Built an interactive Excel sales dashboard with real-time filtering capabilities.

2. Enabled the business to analyze total and category-wise sales trends quickly.

3. Identified top-performing products, best months, and high-revenue store locations.

4. Highlighted actionable insights for product strategy and resource planning.

5. Delivered a ready-to-use, scalable reporting solution that could be easily updated with new sales data, saving manual reporting time.
